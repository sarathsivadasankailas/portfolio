My Travelogue!

The year 2022 was a special year for me. I did my dream travel on 2022 with my family to Europe. Me and my wife along with our parents covered around 7 countries in Europe. We started our trip on May 11th. We took our flight from Kochi to Paris. From France, we travelled through Belgium, Amsterdam, Germany, Switzerland, Italy and Rome. It was a 9 day trip and on May 20th we returned from Milan, Italy to Kochi.

Paris

Paris is the capital and largest city of France with rich culture, history and art. We did a city tour, Seine River cruise, visited the world famous Louvre museum and the iconic Eiffel Tower. The night view of the Eiffel tower was spectacular with sparkling lights.

Belgium - Amsterdam - Cologne

From Paris, we travelled to Amsterdam. On the way we had a stop at Atomium, architectural landmark located in Brussels, Belgium. The main highlight in Amsterdam was the keukenhof garden, one of the world's largest flower gardens, located in Lisse, Netherlands. We had a canal cruise in Amsterdam, a popular and delightful way to explore the picturesque city of Amsterdam. Another highlight in Amsterdam are the Wind Mills. From Amsterdam, we travelled to Switzerland. On the way we stayed at Cologne, Germany. The Cologne Cathedral, is a magnificent Gothic cathedral located in the city of Cologne, Germany.

Switzerland

The main highlight of our trip was Switzerland. Switzerland, located in the heart of Europe, is a land of breathtaking landscapes, cultural richness, and economic prosperity. Switzerland is famous for its stunning Alpine landscapes. Switzerland is dotted with picturesque lakes, including Lake Geneva, Lake Zurich, Lake Lucerne, and Lake Constance. Switzerland's combination of natural beauty, cultural diversity, and economic stability makes it a unique and sought-after destination for tourists and residents alike. Switzerland offers a memorable and enriching experience.
